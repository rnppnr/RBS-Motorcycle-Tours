Guest Relations Manager

Richard, is our guest relations manager and is your friendly point of contact throughout your tour experience. From the moment you inquire about our tours to the time you bid farewell, he is here to assist you with any questions, provide recommendations, and ensure that your individual needs are met. He prides himself on delivering exceptional customer service, creating a warm and inclusive atmosphere, and making you feel like a valued member of our motorcycle tour family.

Lead Tour Guide

Brian is one of our lead tour guides. Our tour guides are the backbone of our operations. With their wealth of experience and intimate understanding of the region, they expertly navigate the roads, curating the most scenic routes and uncovering hidden gems along the way. Their infectious enthusiasm and deep knowledge of the area's history, culture, and natural wonders bring depth and richness to your experience. Simon is our dedicated tour logistics coordinator and ensures that every aspect of your journey is meticulously planned and executed. His attention to detail, organizational skills, and commitment to your comfort and convenience ensure that you can fully immerse yourself in the adventure without worrying about the logistics.
